My Ghanian naming project.

"This project is about the Ghanian
naming culture called Akan
where children are given a day name
which correspond to the day of the week they
were born both male and female.
Most people do Know their birthdays
but might not know the day of the week
that they were born
This project is to help know the day of the
week that they were born depending with the
name they were given "
